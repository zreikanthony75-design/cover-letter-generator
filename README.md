# Cover Letter Generator

A web application that helps you create professional cover letters quickly.

## Features

- **Resume Input**: Paste your resume text or upload a PDF file
- **Customizable**: Add your personal info, company details, and why you want the job
- **4-Part Structure**:
  1. Professional introduction
  2. Experience paragraph highlighting your skills
  3. Company-specific paragraph explaining your interest
  4. Professional conclusion
- **Download**: Save your cover letter as a text file
- **Copy**: One-click copy to clipboard

## How to Run

Simply double-click `index.html` to open it in your web browser. That's it!

Or right-click `index.html` → "Open with" → Choose your browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, etc.)

## How to Use

1. **Step 1**: Provide your resume by either pasting text or uploading a PDF
2. **Step 2**: Enter your contact information
3. **Step 3**: Enter the company name and job title you're applying for
4. **Step 4**: Customize your letter by:
   - Highlighting key skills from your resume
   - Explaining why you want to work at this company
   - Describing why this role excites you
5. Click **Generate Cover Letter**
6. Download or copy your finished cover letter!
